What is Qalb?

Qalb (قلب) means heart in Arabic. This free Islamic dua jar is inspired by the physical jars of rolled-up duas that many Muslims keep at home — a gift of comfort you can reach into whenever your heart needs it. We've made it digital, free, and accessible to anyone, anywhere.

What you'll find here

Every time you open the jar, you receive an authentic Quran verse, a Hadith from Sahih Bukhari or Sahih Muslim, and a dua with transliteration — all chosen to speak directly to what you are going through. Whether you are feeling sadness, anxiety, guilt, loneliness, or gratitude, Islam has words for your heart.

Why authentic sources only?

All hadith in Qalb are sourced exclusively from Sahih al-Bukhari and Sahih Muslim — the two most rigorously authenticated collections in Islamic scholarship. The duas are drawn from the Quran and authenticated sunnah. We believe you deserve accuracy, not just comfort.
