What is Qalb?

Qalb (قلب) means heart in Arabic. This free Islamic dua jar is inspired by the physical jars of rolled-up duas that many Muslims keep at home — a gift of comfort you can reach into whenever your heart needs it. We've made it digital, free, and always available.

What you'll find here

Every time you open the jar, you receive an authentic Quran verse, a Hadith from the six major collections, a Dua with transliteration, Allah's name that speaks to your situation, and adhkar to carry through your day.
