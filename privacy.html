Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Qalb is committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains how we handle information.

What We Collect

Qalb does not require registration or login. Your mood selection is stored only in your browser session and is never sent to any server.

Anonymous Messages

Messages sent via "Share Your Heart" are transmitted through Formspree to a private email. No messages are published, stored in a database, or shared with third parties. They are read only by the Qalb team for the purpose of making dua.

Cookies & Advertising

This website may use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to personalise ads. You can opt out at Google Ads Settings.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdSense — advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • Formspree — anonymous message delivery. Formspree Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — typography loaded from Google servers.
